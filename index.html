ABOUT COACH JASON AUBRY

BUILDING PEOPLE WHO CAN BUILD SOMETHING THAT LASTS.

Coach Jason Aubry is the head football coach at Kentucky Christian University, the author of Clarity of Purpose, and a leadership speaker who helps teams and organizations operate with greater clarity.

His philosophy is simple: the inner reflects the outer. Lasting performance comes from knowing who you are, living by clear standards, and choosing your response when pressure rises.

Jason’s work is grounded in faith, family, service, accountability, and the belief that leadership is first demonstrated by how you live.
